COMPANY LEAVE POLICY

1. Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 working days of annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month.
Employees must submit leave requests at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.

2. Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually.
Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
Sick leave can be carried forward up to 5 days to the next year.

3. Maternity/Paternity Leave
Maternity leave: 16 weeks fully paid
Paternity leave: 4 weeks fully paid
Must provide 30 days notice before expected due date.

4. Emergency Leave
Up to 3 days for family emergencies
Requires manager approval
Documentation may be required

All leave requests should be submitted through the company HR system and require manager approval.
