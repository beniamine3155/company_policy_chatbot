INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY POLICY

1. Acceptable Use
Company equipment for business purposes only
No illegal downloading or file sharing
Personal use limited to breaks and non-work hours

2. Security Requirements
Strong passwords required (min 12 characters)
Multi-factor authentication mandatory
Regular software updates required
No unauthorized software installation

3. Data Protection
Confidential data must be encrypted
No storing company data on personal devices
Regular backup of important files
Report lost devices immediately

4. Network Usage
VPN required for remote access
No connection of personal devices to company network
Bandwidth-intensive activities restricted during work hours

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
