REMOTE WORK POLICY

1. Eligibility
Employees must have completed 6 months of service
Must have satisfactory performance record
Requires manager and HR approval

2. Remote Work Arrangements
Hybrid: 2-3 days remote per week
Full-time remote: Case by case basis
All arrangements reviewed quarterly

3. Requirements
Stable internet connection
Dedicated workspace
Available during core business hours (9 AM - 3 PM)
Must attend mandatory virtual meetings

4. Request Process
Submit Remote Work Request Form
Manager consultation and approval
HR final approval
30-day trial period

5. Equipment
Company provides laptop and necessary software
Employees responsible for internet and utilities
Equipment must be returned upon termination

Remote work privileges may be revoked if performance standards are not met.
